// The Objective

A LEGENDARY SILHOUETTE.
REBORN DIGITAL.

Nike's Air Max needed more than a campaign — it needed a living, breathing digital universe. We built a platform that honours the shoe's legacy while launching it boldly into the future. Immersive 3D configurators, archival storytelling, community-driven content, and real-time colorway customisation converge into an experience as iconic as the visible Air unit itself.

// Design Language

Bold type. Relentless motion.
A palette built from the street up.

We developed a kinetic design system rooted in Air Max DNA — the heat map of the Air unit became a colour story, its geometry a typographic frame. Every scroll gesture echoes the bounce of a sole. Every hover reveals a layer of archive. The result feels like a sneaker that moves through time.
