What started in a waffle iron became a movement. Nike exists to serve human potential.

FROM A
HANDSHAKE
TO A LEGACY.

In 1964, Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman shook hands on a deal to import Japanese running shoes. They sold them from the back of a car. That company became Nike.

Today Nike is the world's leading designer, marketer, and distributor of athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories for a wide variety of sport and fitness activities.
